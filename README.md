<h1>Cycling Company KPI Tracker Dashboard</h1>

<h2>Description</h2>
This project delivers an interactive KPI tracking and performance analytics dashboard designed to monitor the commercial health of a cycling company. The dashboard centralizes key business metrics—including sales volume, revenue, profit, and returns—to provide a clear, real-time view of overall performance and operational efficiency. The solution enables stakeholders to compare regional performance, analyze product-level sales and profitability trends, and identify high-value customers driving revenue growth. Through intuitive filters and drill-down capabilities, users can explore insights by region, product category, customer segment, and time period, supporting data-driven decision-making across sales strategy, inventory planning, and customer retention initiatives.
